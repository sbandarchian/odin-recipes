Buttermilk Pancakes

Easy to make Buttermilk Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Buttermilk
  1. Combine flour,sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl
  2. Beat the buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter together in a seperate bowl. Do not combine the wet and dry ingredients until right before you cook the pancakes.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix with a wooden spoon or fork until the mixtures are just blended. Scoop the batter onto a hot.oil griddle and cook until the pancake is bubbly on the top. Flip with a spatula and cook until both sides are brown.
